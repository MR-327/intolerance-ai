Types of Allergies

Food Allergies

Reaction to certain foods like peanuts, shellfish, or dairy. Example: Peanut allergy causing swelling and difficulty breathing.

Skin Allergies

Caused by irritants like pollen, chemicals, or fabrics. Example: Eczema leading to itchy and inflamed skin.

Respiratory Allergies

Reactions affecting breathing due to dust, pollen, or mold. Example: Hay fever causing sneezing and congestion.

Drug Allergies

Adverse reactions to medications like antibiotics or painkillers. Example: Penicillin allergy causing rashes or anaphylaxis.

Insect Allergies

Reactions to insect stings or bites. Example: Bee sting allergy leading to severe swelling or shock.
